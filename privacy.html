TrendClip - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2025

1. Information We Collect

TrendClip itself does not collect personal data. However, if you connect third-party APIs (e.g., YouTube, TikTok), those services may collect data as per their policies.

2. How We Use Information

API keys and credentials are stored locally on your machine for functionality. They are never transmitted to TrendClip developers.

3. Third-Party Services

TrendClip integrates with platforms such as YouTube, TikTok, and OpenAI. Please review their individual privacy policies.

4. Data Security

Your credentials remain under your control. We recommend rotating keys regularly and following platform security guidelines.

5. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use after updates means acceptance of the revised Policy.
